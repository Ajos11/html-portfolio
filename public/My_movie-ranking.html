My favorite romance movies -- Anuj Joshi

The list includes :

La la land

This movie is particularly visually stunning and includes breathtaking dance sequences and chemistry.

Before Sunrise

The movie feels like a cool breeze flowing through your body and you are in just awe of that feeling that you feel in your body and just want to enjoy that moment till it lasts.

Notting Hill

It's a movie based on experiences between two individuals whose lives are polar opposites according to their statuses in society and how when they meet a void in their life seems to be ful-filled.
